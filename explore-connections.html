This interactive structure maps my artistic practice as a living network of connections. At its center is Musecreate, my studio, branching outward into collections, individual artworks, and the inspirations, materials, techniques, textures, colours, and concepts that shape each piece. Rather than a static portfolio list, this visualization lets you explore how ideas, methods, and works are connected, click on any node to trace its relationships, or search for a specific piece to see how everything links back to the practice as a whole.
