# AI Evolution Timeline

## 1. Rule-Based Beginnings
- Era: 1950s-1960s
- Description: Researchers tried to build intelligence by encoding logic into rules. It worked for tightly defined tasks, but it could not cope with the messy world outside the lab.
- Image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1518770660439-4636190af475?auto=format&fit=crop&w=900&q=80

## 2. Neural Networks
- Era: 1980s-1990s
- Description: The idea of brain-like networks returned. These systems learned patterns from examples and hinted at a more flexible kind of intelligence.
- Image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1531746790731-6c087fecd65a?auto=format&fit=crop&w=900&q=80

## 3. Deep Learning Boom
- Era: 2000s-2010s
- Description: With larger datasets, more compute, and better hardware, AI became dramatically more capable. Image recognition, speech, and translation all accelerated into practical tools.
- Image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1677442136019-21780ecad995?auto=format&fit=crop&w=900&q=80

## 4. Generative AI
- Era: 2020s-Present
- Description: Now AI writes poems, designs images, and helps build software. The field has become less like a calculator and more like a collaborator that keeps changing shape in real time.
- Image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1485827404703-89b55fcc595e?auto=format&fit=crop&w=900&q=80
