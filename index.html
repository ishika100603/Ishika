The Evolution of AI, Still Being Painted

AI did not appear all at once. It arrived in layers: rules, experiments, machines learning from data, and then a strange new era where language itself became a medium. The interface feels half-dried on purpose — like a history that is still settling into shape.

Context

Early AI was built on carefully written logic, as if a mind could be assembled from instructions. Then came neural networks, then deep learning, and now generative systems that can write, draw, and converse. Each step made the future less predictable and more intimate.

Why it feels early

The tools are powerful, yet the boundaries are still shifting. Each new wave changes what we think intelligence is, who gets to shape it, and what the consequences might be. The site is meant to feel like that: not complete, only becoming.
