# Style and Concept

## Visual Direction
This website is designed as a cinematic nighttime experience. The overall mood is dark, immersive, and minimal, with the scene feeling almost black until the car headlights reveal the message.

## Core Concept
The idea is to turn the page into a reveal-driven journey. Instead of showing the text immediately, the experience hides it in darkness and lets the viewer uncover it through motion and light.

## Atmosphere
- Deep night sky with a nearly black environment
- A dark road stretching into the distance
- Dense trees on both sides to create a forest corridor
- Fog that quickly fades distant objects into shadow
- Only the headlights provide a clear visual focus

## Typography
The text is composed of large 3D geometry placed directly on the road. It is intentionally dark and restrained so that it remains unreadable until illuminated by the car lights.

## Car Design
The car is modeled as a simple but believable vehicle using basic primitives. It has a body, roof, windshield, and four wheels, giving it a more realistic form than a cartoon-style object.

## Lighting Approach
The scene uses no ambient light and avoids any emissive text. The only readable illumination comes from the car’s spotlights, making the reveal feel dramatic and precise.

## Interaction Style
The experience is driven by movement. The car follows the mouse position and can also be controlled with arrow keys or WASD, creating a sense of driving through the dark scene while uncovering the hidden phrase.

## Mood and Intent
The design aims to feel mysterious, cinematic, and slightly haunting. The user is not simply reading text; they are actively discovering it by moving through the environment.
